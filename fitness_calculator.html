Are you Exercising Enough To Offset Your Alcohol Consumption?

Try Boozercize To find out

Enter your height, age, gender, and weight to calculate your daily calory requirements. Then select your favorite drink type and enter the number of drinks you consume per week. This will allow the program to calculate the additional calories consumed from alcohol. With this infomation you can load the total calories you need to maintain or lose into the "get recipes" input box to obtain healthy recipes. There is also a list of exercise types that include an approximation of how many calories you will burn per half hour!
